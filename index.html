About Washington First

Washington First is a non-profit research institute dedicated to advancing policies that prioritize Washington families, workers, and communities. We believe in the power of evidence-based policy solutions that strengthen our nation's economy, security, and values.

Our mission is to conduct rigorous research, provide expert analysis, and promote policies that put Washington first in all areas of public life.
